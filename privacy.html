Privacy Policy

Home / Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 10, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how HealthBoom Pharmaceuticals Limited (“HealthBoom”, “we”, “us”, “our”) collects, uses, discloses, and protects your information when you visit our website, purchase products, book appointments, or otherwise interact with us. We process personal data in line with applicable laws, including the Nigeria Data Protection Act (NDPA), the Nigeria Data Protection Regulation (NDPR), and—where applicable—the EU/UK GDPR.

1. Information We Collect

  • Contact & Account Data: name, email, phone number, address.
  • Order & Transaction Data: products purchased, delivery details, receipts.
  • Health/Pharmacy Interactions: information you choose to share with our pharmacists (e.g., allergies, current medications). We request only what is necessary to provide safe services.
  • Technical Data: IP address, device information, pages viewed, cookies/analytics.
  • Marketing Preferences: newsletter opt-ins, communication choices.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide our services (dispensing, retail, consulting) and fulfil orders.
  • Respond to enquiries and provide pharmacy advice.
  • Personalise content, improve our website, and maintain security.
  • Send service messages (e.g., order updates) and—with your consent—marketing communications.
  • Comply with legal obligations and regulatory requirements.

3. Legal Bases for Processing

We process your data based on one or more of: contract (to provide services), consent (e.g., newsletters), legitimate interests (e.g., website security), and legal obligations (e.g., pharmacy record-keeping).

4. Cookies & Analytics

We use essential cookies to run the site and may use analytics to understand performance. You can control cookies through your browser settings. If we use third-party analytics or advertising, we will provide details and options to opt out where required.

5. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal data. We may share limited information with:

  • Service providers that help us operate (e.g., hosting, payment processing, delivery partners) under confidentiality and data protection obligations.
  • Regulators or law enforcement when legally required.
  • Other parties with your explicit consent.

6. International Transfers

If we transfer personal data outside Nigeria, the EU, or the UK, we implement appropriate safeguards (e.g., standard contractual clauses).

7. Data Retention

We keep personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes described above, including meeting legal, accounting, or reporting obligations (e.g., pharmacy records).

8. Your Rights

Subject to applicable law, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your data; the right to object; the right to portability; and the right to withdraw consent at any time (without affecting prior lawful processing). To exercise these rights, contact us using the details below.

9. Children’s Privacy

Our website is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children without appropriate consent and supervision.

10. Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect personal data. However, no online system is completely secure.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will post the new version with an updated “Last updated” date.
