About
HealthBoom
Pharmaceuticals

Healthboom Pharmaceuticals Limited, established in May 2019 and incorporated in Nigeria under RC: 1580966, is a dynamic pharmaceutical company dedicated to advancing healthcare solutions. Licensed by the Pharmacists’ Council of Nigeria, we specialize in the compounding, dispensing, distribution, and sale of high-quality pharmaceutical products to meet the evolving needs of healthcare providers and patients.


At HealthBoom Pharmaceuticals, we are dedicated to advancing healthcare through innovative solutions, high-quality products, and a commitment to excellence. With a focus on research, development, and the distribution of safe and effective pharmaceutical products, we aim to enhance the quality of life for individuals worldwide.


Our team of experts is passionate about delivering cutting-edge medicines and health solutions tailored to meet the evolving needs of patients, healthcare providers, and communities. Guided by our core values of integrity, innovation, and care, we strive to make a meaningful impact on global health and well-being.
